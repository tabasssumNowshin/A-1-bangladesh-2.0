Idea , innovation , vision and challenges in New Bangladesh.

Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Foster a culture of creativity and technological advancement to drive economic growth. Promote research, startups, and digital transformation across all sectors.

Sustainability

Embrace environmentally friendly practices to balance development with ecological preservation. Invest in renewable energy, waste management, and climate-resilient agriculture..

infrastructure

Build modern, resilient infrastructure to support connectivity and economic activities. Focus on transportation, energy grids, and digital networks to enhance efficiency.

Empowerment

Strengthen communities through education, skills development, and gender equality. Provide access to resources and opportunities for individuals to achieve their full potential..

The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.

Green Energy

Investments in solar and wind energy to ensure a sustainable and eco-friendly future.

Modern Infrastructure

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competitiveness.

The fundamental issues facing the world cannot be resolved by merely repeating the same methods that contributed to their creation. True innovation requires a paradigm shift, one that prioritizes systemic solutions over short-term profit. By addressing the underlying causes of social problems through research and innovation, we can foster lasting, sustainable change. In this way, societal transformation becomes the driving force for creating a more balanced and equitable future for humanity."

Dr. Muhammad Yunus

Chief Adviser of the People's Republic of Bangladesh
